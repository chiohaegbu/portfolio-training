My Top Three Movies of all time

For Comedy Romance: One Sweet Day

Played by Sean Connery and Catherine Zeta-Jones,
Released in 2005 and grossed $100 million in the US Box Office

For Fantasy and Adventure: Harry Potter Franchise

One of the greatest fantasy movies ever produced. Based on the book written by J.K Rowleys.
Grossed over 10 billion US dollars since its debut in 1995

Sci-fi: Star wars Franchise

The greatest-of-all-time, Oscar won best picture, best script and screen play. A movie produced and directed by George Lucas
Higest grossed movie of $159 billion in the US Box office.
